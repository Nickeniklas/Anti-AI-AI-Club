Not anti-AI. Anti-bullshit about AI.

Most AI coverage either sells you a dream or sells you dread. We're not doing either. We just track the numbers most people skip: how big the market actually is, what it's doing to jobs, and who's using it for power.

  • Data over vibes. Real numbers with real sources. If we can't back it up, we don't post it.
  • Calm, not doom. We want you informed, not anxious.
  • Critical, not hateful. AI is here to stay. That's exactly why it's worth scrutinising.

Why this exists.

Every AI newsletter seems to be selling a course or selling fear. Nobody was just laying out the numbers for people who are skeptical but not in denial.

This started as a personal pile of interesting articles and a feeling that the public conversation was missing the actual data. Now it's becoming a small club for everyone who feels the same: uneasy about AI, unwilling to pretend it'll vanish, tired of takes with no figures behind them.

We're critical because we take it seriously. We're not hateful because this is the world we live in now. We just think you deserve the real picture.

Questions

Are we actually anti-AI?

No — the name's half a joke. We're critical of the hype and honest about the harms, but we accept AI is here to stay. Clear eyes, not a crusade.

Is this also just doom?

No. We skip the rage-bait. The aim is calm and informed, not anxious.

What makes us different?

Numbers. We lead with data and sources instead of opinions and vibes.
